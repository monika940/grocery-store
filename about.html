📖 Our Story

From Farm to Your Table

GArocery Store was founded in 2020 with a simple mission: make fresh, organic groceries accessible to every household in India. We believe everyone deserves to eat healthy without breaking the bank.

We partner directly with over 200 local farms across Punjab, Maharashtra, and Karnataka to source the freshest produce — cutting out the middleman and passing the savings on to you.

Today, we serve over 10,000 happy customers across major cities, delivering fresh groceries in 30 minutes or less. Our commitment to quality, sustainability, and customer happiness drives everything we do.
