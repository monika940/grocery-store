❓ FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How fast is your delivery? ▼

We deliver within 30 minutes for orders placed in our service areas. For remote locations, delivery may take up to 60 minutes.

Is the produce really organic? ▼

Yes! All our produce is certified organic by FSSAI and sourced directly from verified farms. We lab-test every batch.

What is your return policy? ▼

If you're unhappy with any product, contact us within 24 hours of delivery. We offer full refunds or replacements, no questions asked.

Do you offer free delivery? ▼

Yes! Orders above ₹499 get free delivery. Otherwise, a flat ₹49 delivery fee applies.

What payment methods are accepted? ▼

We accept UPI, credit/debit cards, net banking, and cash on delivery. All digital payments are secured with SSL encryption.
